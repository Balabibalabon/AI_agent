# One-Day Meal Plan for Marathon Training Athlete

Here is a one-day meal plan for a high-endurance athlete in marathon training:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3200 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (120g), Fat (85g), Carbs (450g)

**Meal Plan:**

* **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with banana (150g), honey (30g), and whole milk (240g). Side of whole wheat toast (60g) with almond butter (20g).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 95g

* **Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM):** Sports drink (500ml) with electrolytes and granola bar (50g).
    * Protein: 6g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 65g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Grilled chicken breast (150g) with brown rice (150g cooked), steamed sweet potato (200g), and mixed vegetables (100g) drizzled with olive oil (15ml).
    * Protein: 40g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 85g

* **Pre-Training Snack (4:00 PM):** Energy bar (60g) and apple (180g) with peanut butter (25g).
    * Protein: 10g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 70g

* **Dinner (7:30 PM):** Baked salmon (160g) with pasta (120g dry weight), marinara sauce (150g), and steamed broccoli (150g). Olive oil drizzle (10ml).
    * Protein: 35g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 95g

* **Evening Recovery Snack (9:30 PM):** Greek yogurt (200g) with berries (120g) and granola (40g).
    * Protein: 15g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 50g

**Hydration Strategy:** Consume 2.5-3 liters of water throughout the day, plus electrolyte drinks during/around training sessions.
